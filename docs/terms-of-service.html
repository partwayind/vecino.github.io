5. Closed-Loop Job Flow

Each job moves through these stages: Post → Negotiate → Locked Agreement → Workspace → Mutual Completion → Job Seal → Vouch.

  • The agreement becomes immutable once both sides accept ("locked agreement"). Mid-job changes require a Follow-Up Job, not edits to the original.
  • Funds release only on mutual completion (both sides confirm) or via auto-release after a 5-day window with no dispute.
  • A Job Seal is created on closure: a cryptographically signed, tamper-evident archive containing the agreement, evidence, and payment record. Job Seals are immutable and retained indefinitely.

6. Disputes
