1. Introduction

Vecino ("we," "our," "us," or the "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our platform. Vecino — Spanish for "neighbor" — is a structured local services platform connecting Neighbors (those posting work and those offering their skills) for real-world contract work, including handyman work, yard projects, home improvement, and similar community-based services.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and otherwise handle your information when you use our mobile application ("App"), website, and related services (collectively, the "Services"). Throughout this document, "Neighbor" refers to any person using Vecino, whether posting work or offering their skills.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our practices, please do not use our Services. By accessing or using Vecino, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by this Privacy Policy.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Precise GPS coordinates when you search for nearby work or Neighbors — used on your device for distance calculations and not stored on our servers

  • A 5-mile privacy floor is applied to all displayed distances (distances under 5 miles are shown as "< 5 mi")
  • Address autocomplete: when you type an address into Vecino (e.g., setting your work location, creating a post, or entering an address during sign-up), the partial text you type is forwarded through our server to Mapbox's Geocoding API so we can return address suggestions. Only the typed text — and, when available, an approximate location bias — is sent to Mapbox; your account identity, email, and precise GPS location are not

4. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction:

4.1 Encryption

  • In Transit: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using TLS/SSL protocol
  • At Rest: Sensitive personal information (name, phone, address) is encrypted using AES-256-GCM encryption
  • End-to-End Encrypted Communications: Chat messages between Neighbors are encrypted client-side using X25519 key exchange and AES-256-GCM — they are stored encrypted on our servers and can only be decrypted on your device
  • Job Seals: Completed job records are cryptographically signed using HMAC-SHA256 to create tamper-evident archives. These seals cannot be altered after the job closes

4.2 Key Management

Private encryption keys are stored securely in:

  • iOS: Apple Keychain
  • Android: Android KeyStore

Keys are never transmitted to servers and are inaccessible to our team.

4.3 Access Controls

  • Row-level security policies enforce database access controls
  • Employee access to user data is restricted to necessary job functions only
  • All data access is logged and monitored

4.4 Payment Security

Important: Vecino does NOT store credit card information. All payment processing is handled by Stripe, a PCI DSS Level 1 compliant payment processor. We only receive transaction confirmations.

4.5 Limitations

While we implement comprehensive security measures, no system is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your information. If you believe your account has been compromised, contact us immediately at [INSERT CONTACT EMAIL].

6.2 Deletion

You can request deletion of your account from within the app: Settings → Delete my account. Upon request, your account enters a 30-day recovery window. During the window:

  • You are signed out of all devices.
  • Logging back in will prompt you to either recover the account or stay deleted.
  • If you do not recover within 30 days, your account is finalized and cannot be restored.

After the 30-day window:

  • Profile contents are scrubbed: username, avatar, encrypted contact info, payment-method references, encryption keys, and notification preferences are cleared.
  • Account email is replaced with a non-deliverable tombstone in the authentication system so the address cannot be reused for login or receive mail.
  • Push notification binding is detached.
  • What is preserved (legally required or load-bearing for other neighbors' records): job offers, payment logs, referral payouts, Job Seal archives, chat messages, and vouches you wrote. These records remain intact, but anywhere your name or avatar would have appeared, the UI will display "Deleted Neighbor."

If you are unable to access the app, you can request deletion via the contact email listed at vecino.io/account-deletion.html.

6.3 Opt-Out Preferences

6.4 Do Not Track
